About Heat Alert

Heat Alert shows how hot the Earth's surface is, anywhere, on any day since 2012.

Map layer

The colored map comes from NASA's Terra satellite (MODIS instrument), delivered through NASA GIBS. It updates daily with roughly a two-day processing delay, and its archive runs back to May 2012. Cloud cover can leave gaps in a given day's image.

Click to inspect

Clicking the map queries NASA POWER for the skin temperature at that exact point, along with its history back to 1981. Pin up to three locations to compare their trends on one chart.

Built with

Leaflet for the map, Chart.js for trends, and basemap tiles from CARTO.

No account, no tracking, no backend — every request goes straight from your browser to NASA's public APIs.
